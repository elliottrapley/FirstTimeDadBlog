Incmoming Baby Stuff!

2nd September 2020
19:35 BST

I'm incredibly lucky to have such a great support network around us. Sarah's parents have put dibs on buying their grandchild a pushchair. My Dad called me to ask what he could buy and Sarah and I suggested he can purchase the cot of he would like. Soon after, my Step-Mum sent us a screenshot of a white cot. It was a sleigh-styled cot and Sarah fell in love with it. In the next couple of days, my dad called me to say he's coming over to drop the cot off.

My sister called me and said that she went shopping and bought a Dumbo onesie outfit, a Winnie the Pooh blanket and a selection of bibs. Can you tell she's just as excited?

My mum sent Sarah and I a WhatsApp message to say that she's transferring us some money for us to spend on getting bits for the baby. We spent some of this on items for decorating the baby's bedroom. We also bought a changing unit. Most of the furniture stuff we bought from Facebook Market Place as they're still in pretty good condition. A friend of mine who I was used to work with offered us a bottle steralising machine and a Mamma's and Pappa's swing chair thing. Again, in really good condition. Sarah and I thought that we could save so much money if we bought most items second-hand.

Having this support is amazing. I'm very grateful as I know there are many first time parents who may not have the support that we've received and have to do it alone. This is the mindset that I'm trying to have which is just to be grateful for what we have. We own our own home, we're both in secure jobs and we have wonderful family and friends.
